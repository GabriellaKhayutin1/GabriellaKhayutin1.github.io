My study choice, why I choose ICT?

I am very motivated for ICT because it was always a dream of mine to have the possibility to travel and to work anywhere I want. This sphere gives me the opportunity to do what I love while traveling.

In addition, I am very curious about all the technology around us. While also I am a very logical person, so I find ICT to be the best subject for me to study. It has all the things I like and definitely a subject that has a future .
